How To Play

Create

To begin the process, the users will be directed to the OneFirmMarket to design their own virtual law firm with an initial investment fund.

Compete

Once users have created their virtual law firms, a newsfeed relevant to their selected practices can be viewed on the dashboard under OneFirmNews.

Refine

After a month of a practice ownership, users are awarded with the ability to sell practices to other users, either through a trade or an auction, in a transfer market window style on OneFirmMarket.

Win

The success of the users' virtual law firms are reflected by their position on The League Table at the end of the year. The top three virtual law firms, based on the overall points achieved will be recognized by OneFirmVision and receive an award for their success.
